Roof Coatings & Restoration

Energy-efficient coatings and restoration that extend the life of commercial and flat roofs.

You don't always need a full replacement. For many commercial, flat, and metal roofs, a professional coating or restoration seals leaks, reflects Texas heat, and adds years of service life — at a fraction of the cost of tearing off.

Our restoration systems seal seams and penetrations, stop existing leaks, and lay down a reflective membrane that lowers cooling costs and protects the roof beneath. It's a smart, sustainable option that keeps your building watertight and your energy bills down.

We'll assess whether coating or replacement is truly the right call — and give you the honest answer, not the most expensive one.

What's Included

  • Reflective, energy-efficient coatings
  • Flat & low-slope roof restoration
  • Commercial & metal roof systems
  • Seam, seal & leak repair
  • Silicone & acrylic coating systems
  • Extended roof life & lower cooling costs

Every coatings & restoration project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate. Call 210-623-1101 or request one online and we'll take it from there.
