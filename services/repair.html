Roof Repair

Fast, reliable roof repair for leaks, storm damage, and everyday wear.

When your roof fails, you need it fixed right — and fast. Sandoval Roofing Services Inc. responds quickly to leaks, missing shingles, and storm damage, restoring your roof's integrity before the next Texas downpour.

We track leaks to their true source instead of just patching the symptom, so the fix actually holds. From a handful of blown-off shingles to hail-damaged flashing and valleys, our crews make clean, lasting repairs that blend into your existing roof.

Storm damage? We offer emergency tarping to stop the bleeding and can document everything for your insurance claim.

What's Included

  • Leak detection & permanent repair
  • Missing & damaged shingle replacement
  • Hail & wind storm-damage repair
  • Flashing, valley & penetration repair
  • Emergency tarping & water stoppage
  • Insurance claim documentation

Every roof repair project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate. Call 210-623-1101 or request one online and we'll take it from there.
