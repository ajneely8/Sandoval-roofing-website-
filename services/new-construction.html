New Construction Roofing

Precision roofing systems for new residential and commercial builds across San Antonio & South Texas.

Starting fresh gives us the chance to do it perfectly. Sandoval Roofing Services Inc. partners with homeowners, builders, and general contractors to engineer new-construction roofs that are watertight, code-compliant, and built to last from the very first course.

We coordinate directly with your build schedule so your roof goes on right, on time, and without holding up the rest of the project. From blueprint review to final inspection, our certified crews handle every detail — proper decking, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and the finished roofing system in the material of your choice.

Shingle, standing-seam metal, or tile — we help you weigh cost, curb appeal, energy efficiency, and longevity, then install a system backed by manufacturer and workmanship warranties.

What's Included

  • Residential & commercial new builds
  • Architectural shingle, metal & tile systems
  • Blueprint review & full code compliance
  • Builder & general-contractor coordination
  • Proper ventilation & flashing detailing
  • Manufacturer + workmanship warranties
